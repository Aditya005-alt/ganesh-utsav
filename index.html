Story of Lord Ganesha

Lord Ganesha, the beloved son of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati, was created by Parvati from clay to guard her chambers. When Shiva unknowingly tried to enter, Ganesha stopped him. Ganesha is also remembered for writing the great epic Mahabharata. Sage Vyasa dictated the verses, and Ganesha agreed to write them down with the condition that Vyasa should recite without pause. With his broken tusk, Ganesha penned down one of the greatest scriptures in Indian history.
